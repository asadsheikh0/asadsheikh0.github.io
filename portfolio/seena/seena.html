responsive web design

interaction design studio i, fall 2019

team members: asad sheikh, qijia li, jinyi yi, david wang

overview

This project in Interaction Design Studio I was about creating a responsive web design for a given domain. We were given the domain of emergency response and chose to focus on the paramedic to hospital handoff of patients.

My role in the group revolved around managing the details of our solution and how to incorporate those into the design, as well as helping design the interface of our website.

problem statement

“Latency, low efficiency, and miscommunication existed in current patient handling process, which mainly relies on verbal communication.”
